# How to Join a Loop

Joining a loop in HITL.sh allows you to start reviewing requests and contributing to human-in-the-loop workflows. This guide walks you through the process of setting up your account and joining your first review loop.

## Prerequisites

Before you can join a loop, you'll need:

- ✅ An invitation email from a loop administrator
- ✅ The HITL.sh mobile app installed on your device
- ✅ A stable internet connection
- ✅ Basic understanding of the review process

<Card title="Download the App" icon="smartphone" href="https://my.hitl.sh">
Get the HITL.sh mobile app for iOS and Android devices.
</Card>

## Step 1: Receive Invitation

### Invitation Email

After downloading the app, you can join loops using multiple methods:

<Frame>
<img src="/images/join_loop.PNG" alt="HITL.sh mobile app join loop screen with code input and QR scan options" />
</Frame>

**Join Methods Available:**
- **Loop Code**: Enter a 6-digit code provided by the loop administrator
- **QR Code Scan**: Use your camera to scan a QR code
- **Direct Links**: Tap invitation links shared via email or messaging
- **Social Sign-in**: Continue with Google, Apple, or email authentication

<Info>
Invitations typically expire after 7 days. If your invitation expires, contact the loop administrator for a new one.
</Info>

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

### Basic Information

Set up your reviewer profile:

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Personal Details">
- **Full Name**: Your complete name as it should appear
- **Email**: Your email address for notifications
- **Phone**: Optional phone number for urgent alerts
- **Profile Photo**: Upload a professional photo (optional)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Expertise Areas">
- **Primary Skills**: Your main areas of expertise
- **Secondary Skills**: Additional skills and knowledge
- **Experience Level**: Years of experience in relevant fields
- **Languages**: Languages you can review content in
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Availability">
- **Time Zone**: Your current time zone
- **Working Hours**: When you're typically available
- **Response Preferences**: How quickly you can respond

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Invitation Link Not Working">
- Check if the invitation has expired
- Ensure you're using the correct device
- Try copying and pasting the link
- Contact the loop administrator
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="App Won't Install">
- Check device compatibility
- Ensure sufficient storage space
- Try restarting your device
- Contact app store support
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't Complete Profile">
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the app
- Clear app cache and restart
- Contact technical support
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Training Not Loading">
- Check internet connection
- Try accessing from different network
- Clear browser cache if using web version
- Contact training administrator
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
